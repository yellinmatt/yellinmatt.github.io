What this is

Greatest Bits is a free, non-commercial, fan-made archive and editorial project about stand-up comedy. It isn't affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any comedian, network, studio, or platform it indexes.

The original writing here, the rankings, the analysis, the editorial, is the project's own. Please don't republish it wholesale. A link back is always welcome.

Getting it right matters. If anything is wrong or mis-credited, or you're a rights holder who wants something removed, get in touch and it's fixed or taken down promptly, no questions asked.

The site is provided as is. We aim for accuracy and never knowingly fabricate a link, quote, date, or attribution, but the catalogue is a work in progress and may contain errors or gaps. Rankings are one editor's opinion, offered as such.

These terms may change as the project grows, especially before any accounts, contributions, or community features launch, which will get their own reviewed terms. Continuing to use the site means accepting the current version.

How it works

Method & Sources

Two ideas run the project. Discovery is systematic: we catalogue broadly first so judgment works on a complete board, never a blank page. Ranking is taste-driven: the lists are ordered by hand, list by list, and everything stays re-rankable as the canon grows.

Sources are official only. Comic photographs come from Wikimedia Commons (CC BY-SA); posters from TMDB; clips are embedded from official YouTube and Spotify, or linked to the special itself. Nothing is hosted here, and every fact is meant to trace to a real source.

A full written methodology, the ranking scorecard, and a complete source list are being written. Coming soon

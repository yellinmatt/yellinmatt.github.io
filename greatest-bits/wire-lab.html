Concept A

Dispatch Rail — horizontal scroll of cards

A side-scrolling rail of bold dispatch cards. Compact vertically, holds a lot, feels alive and browsable. Best if the section should feel like a living feed you flick through.

Concept B

Marquee Board — a comedy "departure board"

A dark split-flap board: each item a row with a category "gate," monospace dateline, and a strong headline. The most distinctive and ownable — reads like the marquee outside a club. Best if the section should feel like a signature fixture.

Concept C

Lead + Briefs — an editorial front page

One big lead dispatch beside a tight stack of one-line briefs. The most "curated/edited" feel — signals judgment and hierarchy. Best if you want it to read like a front page rather than a feed.

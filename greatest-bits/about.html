The creed
  1. I

    Discovery is systematic

    The catalogue is filled broadly first, so judgment always works on a complete board, never a blank page. Obscure but important bits are logged with the gap flagged, never quietly dropped.

  2. II

    Ranking is taste-driven

    The lists are ordered by hand, one placement at a time. No algorithm, no crowd poll behind the numbers: a personal canon, argued and owned, with the reasoning shown.

  3. III

    The list is alive

    Comics and bits are added as they are watched and reconsidered, and everything stays re-rankable as the canon grows. A record kept in motion, not a monument poured once.

  4. IV

    Always back to the source

    Greatest Bits indexes the work; it never passes it off as its own. Nothing is hosted here. We prefer the artist's own channel and the major comedy networks; where no official upload exists, we embed long-standing public uploads the rights holders have left up. Every page points you back to the artist, and anything is taken down on request.

  5. V

    Never fabricate

    No invented link, quote, date, or attribution. Where something can't be verified, the gap is shown plainly rather than papered over.

bit/bɪt/n.

the atom of stand-up — a single routine, joke, story, or sustained run that stands on its own. The project casts wide on type: built routines, long-form stories, one-liners, legendary crowd work, callbacks, act-outs.

"Carlin's Seven Words. Pryor's heart-attack act-out. A tight Hedberg one-liner run."

Two entities support every bit and link back to it: the comic who performs it, and the special — album, set, or broadcast — it came from. The model is built to stretch, so new kinds of entries can be added later without a rebuild.

Every photograph, poster, and clip belongs to its owner and appears only to identify the artist's work and point you to the source — fair use for criticism, commentary, and reference. If something is wrong, or you'd like a clip, photo, or page removed, get in touch and we'll take it down promptly, no questions asked.

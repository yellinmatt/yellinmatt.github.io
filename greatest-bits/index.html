The projectMethod & sources
A non-commercial, fan-made index of stand-up, in progress. Photographs, posters, and clips are used to identify and credit each artist's work and point you to the official source — all rights remain with their owners and nothing is hosted here. Comic photos via Wikimedia Commons (CC BY-SA); posters via TMDB; clips embedded from official YouTube / Spotify or linked to the official special. Something wrong or want it removed? Get in touch and we'll take it down.

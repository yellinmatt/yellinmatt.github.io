Follow & Support

Greatest Bits is a passion project, made for love rather than money. The best way to support it right now is simple: use it, and help make it more accurate.

There's no paywall, no ads, and nothing to buy. As the project grows there will be more ways to follow along and pitch in: a newsletter, social channels, and eventually a place to log your own picks. Those are on the way. For now, here's how to help.

Every photo, poster, and clip belongs to its owner and appears here only to identify the artist's work and point you to it: fair use for criticism, commentary, and reference. Nothing is hosted on this site. Photos come from Wikimedia Commons (CC BY-SA), posters from TMDB, and clips embed from YouTube and Spotify, official channels where they exist and long-standing public uploads where they don't.

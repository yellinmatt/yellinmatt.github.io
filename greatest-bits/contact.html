The projectMethod & sources
Greatest Bits is a non-commercial, fan-made archive, and a work in progress. Every photograph, poster, and clip belongs to its owner and appears here only to identify the artist's work and point you to the official source. Nothing is hosted on this site: comic photos come from Wikimedia Commons (CC BY-SA), posters from TMDB, and clips are embedded from official YouTube and Spotify or linked to the special itself. If something is wrong, or you would like it removed, get in touch and we will take it down.

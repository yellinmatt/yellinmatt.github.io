The projectMethod & sources
A non-commercial, fan-made archive, in progress. Every photograph, poster, and clip belongs to its owner and appears here only to identify the artist's work and point you to the source — fair use for criticism, commentary, and reference. Nothing is hosted on this site: comic photos via Wikimedia Commons (CC BY-SA), posters via TMDB, clips embedded from YouTube and Spotify — official channels (the artist's own, Netflix Is A Joke, HBO, Comedy Central, Just For Laughs, Team Coco, and so on) where they exist, otherwise long-standing public uploads the rights holders have left up. If something is wrong, or you'd like a clip, photo, or page removed, get in touch and we'll take it down promptly, no questions asked.

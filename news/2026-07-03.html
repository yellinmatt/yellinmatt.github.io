June hiring comes in at half of forecast, and a September Fed hike falls off the table

American employers added just 57,000 jobs in June, the Bureau of Labor Statistics said Thursday in a report pulled forward a day for the July 4 holiday, against a consensus near 115,000 and the weakest gain in four months.
